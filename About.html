About This App

>

The Weather app will automatically keep track of the climate and temperature once you enter the location..
However, you can see other locations you might want to keep track of.
Whether you're traveling or simply have family, friends, or colleagues around the world, you easily keep all the information you want, all available at a glance.

The Weather Channel is incorporated with satellite and Doppler radar to provide highly accurate weather information on your Computers & smartphones. It uses Ultra Local True Point Forecasting technology to find the best weather reports. It has over 200 meteorologists gathering and processing information.
